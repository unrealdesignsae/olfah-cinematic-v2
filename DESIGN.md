# Design System

## Direction

A single-screen cinematic real-estate experience inspired by the pacing and interaction model of Sakura Gardens, translated into Olfah's forest identity. The home screen is immersive and minimal; detail lives in large, focused modal states.

## Color

- Forest ink: `#132019`
- Canopy: `#263d28`
- Warm limestone: `#f2eee3`
- Olive line: `#8e8a61`
- Soft ivory: `#faf7ef`
- Terracotta accent: `#b87352`

## Type

- Display: Tenor Sans, ultra-light and spacious
- Accent: Bodoni Moda italic
- Interface/body: Manrope

## Interaction

- Floating rounded navigation on desktop
- Full-viewport hero with slow crossfades
- Large rounded overlays for project details and masterplan
- Dark cinematic overlays for gallery, film, developer and panorama
- Motion uses opacity and transform only; reduced-motion settings are respected
- Controls have visible focus states and minimum 44px touch targets

## Imagery

Use only supplied Olfah renders. Preserve architecture, composition and perspective. Maintain a soft, natural, warm-to-blue-hour grade with realistic landscape color. Do not add grain, noise or crosshatching.
