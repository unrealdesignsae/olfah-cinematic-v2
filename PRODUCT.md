# Product Context

Olfah V2 is a cinematic, single-screen property experience for prospective homeowners and investors considering Alef Group's Olfah community in Muwaileh, Sharjah.

The experience must make Olfah feel rooted, architectural and serene while keeping the complete project story accessible through focused full-screen panels. The interactive masterplan is the primary discovery tool and connects directly to eight 360° panoramas.

Avoid generic real-estate portal patterns, corporate blue, neon color, noisy texture, crosshatching, excessive glass effects, architectural alterations, or unsupported sales claims. Preserve all original renders and factual project data.

The primary conversion is registering interest with Alef Group. Secondary actions are exploring the masterplan, opening panoramas, viewing the gallery and downloading the brochure.
